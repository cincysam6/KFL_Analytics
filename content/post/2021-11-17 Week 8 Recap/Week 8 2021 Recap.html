Expected Wins power rankings through week 8

Week 8 sees the Ohio and Indiana division pull away with two top tier teams. Coming off there big trades the Supermarket Studs sit in our top spot with 5.3 expected wins. The Slugs and Kraken Tie for second with 5.1 and Swab rounds out the top 4 at 5. The Unlucky Ducks are at a full 3.7 wins under expected with only 1 win on the season but 4.7 expected wins. It is truly shaping up to be one of the toughest luck seasons for the Ducks. Scott’s Titans on the other hand, enjoy a division lead despite a meager 3.8 expected wins on the season.
