Week 2 Recap

The Bluecats get another fortunate win putting up less than 80 against the Slugs who came back down to earth this week with a poor performance. The incorrectly named Unlucky Ducks got a very lucky win against an opponent who can’t set their lineup and still almost lost. Meanwhile in the Glum division Elliot’s Last Good Tight End put up a smash victory with almost a record setting week and the Titan’s and Onionhead’s had a competitive high scoring match-up making the Glum division look strong to start the season. Rounding the week out the Sunday Winner’s put up an impressive performance against the Monarchs who also came back down to earth with a poor 68 point performance and the Supermarket Studs and looking like the Supermarket Duds as they lost to Kraken who were not much better.

Expected Wins power rankings through week 2 2022

The big week from Elliot’s Last Good Tight Ends (LGTE) was one of the highest we’ve ever seen in the KFL. This basically guaranteed a win posting an expected win value of 0.99 and catupalting them to the top of our leaderboard. Former league leader the Slugs fall to second with a big gap between them and LGTE. The Glum division looks like they may be tough this year with the Onionheads and Titans rounding out the top 4. We have two 2-0 teams who have benefited from getting some low scoring opponents. Nick’s (Un)Lucky ducks have put up a meager 0.6 expected wins and the Bluecats aren’t much better at 0.7. We will see if they can keep this luck streak alive or if they will inevitably come crashing down to earth. On the other end of the spectrum, the Monarchs should have a win by now ranking 7th with 1.01 expected wins, but sadly sit at 0-2 and will look to reverse their luck against the Bluecats next week.
