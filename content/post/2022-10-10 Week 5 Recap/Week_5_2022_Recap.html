Week 5 Recap

Nick’s “UN” Lucky Ducks are anything but and they should find their way to a casino shortly with the heater they are on. Not only do they get another win, but they win a tie-breaker off a miracle of a game by TE Travis Kelce. The Kraken put up another big win and keep pace right on the Duck’s heels. Meanwhile, LGTE win a slugfest against the woefully unlucky Monarchs who seem destined to face 100+ point opponents week after week. The Bluecats got a win against the Titans to take the division lead. In a battle of the basement, the GMO’s eked out a win against the Tenderloins.

Standings through Week 5

We continue to see the haves and have-nots in the KFL spread further apart. We have 6 1-4 teams who need to turn it around next week or pack the season in. Meanwhile Nick’s “UN”Lucky Ducks now are the sole undefeated team at 5-0 after another win. The Onionhead’s and LGTE share the lead in the Glum Division at 4-1 and the Bluecat’s take sole possession of first at 4-1 as the Slugs dropped a heartbreaker to fall to 3-2.

One of these 4 win or better teams is going to miss the playoffs which will be a harsh beat this year given the lack of mediocrity in the league thus far.
