Week 6 Recap

This week was a low scoring affair with zero games above 100 points. The “UN”Lucky ducks finally getting knocked off by the newly named Death Monarchs restoring a bit of parity to the league. LGTE continue their slide with a loss to the Kraken who now share the division lead with Nick. The Tenderloin’s continue to lose as they fell to the Titan’s and the GMO’s found a way to win over the Sunday Winner’s despite only putting up 63. Finally, the Slugs had a chance to catch up to the Bluecats, but lost to the Onionheads who take sole lead in the Glum division.
